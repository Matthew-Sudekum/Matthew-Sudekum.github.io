I started off like most self taught web devs, I found the wisdom of an online course. After completing the Complete Web Developer course by Stefan Mischook, I built a full stack web app.

Now by this point I had heard of frameworks and how they are used by everyone. So I made the all to common mistake of delving into them before I had solidified my vanilla JavaScript. I made 2 projects using Angular, found here and on my GitHub.

Now, thanks to the wisdom of fellow web devs with jobs in the industry, I decided to steer my journey back to the bare-bones graveyard of JavaScript and web development.

I am now making sites with much better feel to them without nearly as much complexity. Let this site be your guide to my past, present, and future.
